Privacy Policy — StreakLife

Last updated: May 18, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the StreakLife app ("Application") created by Rosu Studio ("Service Provider").

Information We Collect

When you use StreakLife, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: If you sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address.
  • Usage data: We collect anonymous analytics data about how you use the app (screens visited, features used) via Google Firebase Analytics.
  • Habit data: Your habits, streaks, XP, and progress are stored locally on your device and/or in our secure cloud database (Firebase Firestore) if you are signed in.
  • Advertising ID: Firebase Analytics may use your device's Advertising ID to provide analytics and improve app performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Application
  • To save and sync your progress across devices (if signed in)
  • To analyze app usage and improve user experience
  • To display your username on leaderboards (if signed in)

Third Party Services

StreakLife uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, and analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — For account authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics for Firebase — For usage analytics.

Data Storage

If you use the app without an account, all data is stored locally on your device only. If you sign in with Google, your progress is also stored securely in Firebase Firestore servers managed by Google.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data may be shared with Google as part of Firebase and Google Sign-In services as described above.

Children's Privacy

StreakLife is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Users must be at least 13 years old to use this Application.
