Our Story

Small studio.
Big intentions.

Rosu Studio is a one-person indie mobile studio based in Romania. We build apps that are beautiful, fast, and actually useful — one feature at a time.

Why we exist

Apps should earn your attention.

Too many apps are cluttered, distracting, and hollow. We build the opposite: apps that are quiet, beautiful, and deeply useful. Every pixel earns its place.

Rosu Studio started with a personal frustration — no habit tracker felt right. Either they were too simple or gamified in a cheap way. StreakLife is our answer: a habit tracker that genuinely respects your time and intelligence, while making progress feel tangible and rewarding.

Our principles

How we build.

History

How we got here.

24
Late 2024
The idea

Frustrated with existing habit trackers, started sketching what StreakLife could look like. A habit tracker that actually gamifies progress — no subscriptions, no fluff.

25
Early 2025
First build

Built the first version of StreakLife in Flutter. Core loop: habits → streaks → XP. Firebase backend, Google Sign-In, basic Neumorphic UI. Dogfooding it daily.

↑
Mid 2025
Avatar system

Added the layered avatar system — PNG layers for outfit, hair, accessories. Custom Python scripts to generate the assets. The Flames currency shop launched.

🚀
2026
Rosu Studio founded

StreakLife hits a polish milestone. Rosu Studio is formalized. Website launches. More apps in the pipeline.
